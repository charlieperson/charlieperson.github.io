Hi there, I'm Charlie Person!

I am attending Hack Reactor in Austin, Texas. It's a sixteen week software engineering bootcamp that specializes in JavaScript. This is the second bootcamp I have attended. I've learned that during these high intensity programs, it can feel like trying to drink knowledge from a fire hose. When I pack tons of information into my head in a short amount of time, it's difficult to be sure of what is actually sticking, and what isn't. To help with this, I will attempt to write one blog post everyday to solidify my learnings from that day, as well as help find, and be more aware of the gaps in my knowledge.
